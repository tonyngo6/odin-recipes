Only the finest ingredients should be used, but let's skip the trash fermenting part this time.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, etc.)
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn (optional)
  • Olive oil for drizzling

Recipe Intstructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature recommended for your pizza dough.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. If using store-bought dough, follow the package instructions.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread a generous amount of tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, ensuring it covers the entire surface.
  6. Add your desired toppings, such as pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, or any other ingredients you prefer.
  7. If desired, tear some fresh basil leaves and scatter them over the pizza.
  8. Drizzle a little olive oil over the pizza to enhance the flavors.
  9. Carefully place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake according to the dough's instructions or until the crust turns golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  10. Once cooked, remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a minute or two.
  11. Slice the pizza into wedges or squares, and serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza! It's perfect for sharing with friends or as a cozy dinner option. Feel free to customize the toppings and add your favorite seasonings to make it your own.
