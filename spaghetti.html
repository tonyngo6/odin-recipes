SPAGHETTIIIIIIII

Here's a classic spaghetti recipe for you:

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces spaghetti pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 pound ground beef or ground turkey
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper (or to taste)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving
  • Chopped fresh parsley, for garnish (optional)

Recipe Intstructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and chopped onion to the skillet. Saut√© for about 2-3 minutes until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
  4. Add the ground beef or turkey to the skillet. Cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until browned and cooked through.
  5. Drain any excess fat from the skillet, if necessary.
  6. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.
  7. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for about 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  8. Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  9. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the sauce. Toss everything together until the pasta is well coated with the sauce.
  10. Remove from heat and let it sit for a minute to allow the flavors to blend.
  11. Serve the spaghetti hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh parsley for garnish, if desired.

Enjoy your delicious homemade spaghetti! It's a classic comfort food that never disappoints. Feel free to add your favorite vegetables or spices to make it even more flavorful.
